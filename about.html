About Windsor Terrace Vets

Windsor Terrace Vets is a family-run practice that prides itself on providing tailored, compassionate care for every patient. We treat your pets like family — listening, educating, and partnering with you to make the best decisions for your companion's health.

Meet our team

Dr. Mary Nasr

Dr. Mary Nasr is a Staten Island native who graduated from Rutgers University with a Bachelor’s in Animal Science, and Cornell University College of Veterinary Medicine. Dr. Nasr completed an internship at Oradell Animal Hospital and has since worked as both an emergency veterinarian and general practitioner. Dr. Nasr has a passion for surgery and exotic animal medicine, and has her own little turtle who greets her every morning with excitement and plenty of splashes. Outside of work, Dr. Nasr enjoys a good book and dabbles in music, painting, woodburning, and other types of art.

Dr. Nagy Nasr

Dr. Nagy Nasr graduated from veterinary school in Egypt in 1984, and after immigrating to the US, completed the ECFVG (Education Commission for Foreign Veterinary Graduates) at Cornell University College of Veterinary Medicine in 2001. With many years of experience under his belt in both large and small animal medicine, and in multiple countries that span Africa, Europe, and South America, Dr. Nagy Nasr acquired Windsor Terrace Vets in 2004 and has been serving the community ever since.
