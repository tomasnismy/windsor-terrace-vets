Important Links & Resources

ASPCA — Toxic Plants for Dogs

Always double-check your house plants to make sure they are not toxic to your dogs. If your dog eats a toxic plant, contact us or the ASPCA Poison Control Hotline immediately.

ASPCA — Toxic Plants for Cats

Check this list to keep your cats safe from harmful plants. If your cat eats a toxic plant, contact us or the ASPCA Poison Control Hotline immediately.

FDA — Grain-Free Foods & Canine DCM

There is currently research being done with regards to grain free foods and heart disease in dogs. Please talk to one of our vets regarding your pet’s diet.

OSU — Indoor Pet Initiative

If, during your appointment, one of our vets mentions the “indoor pet initiative website”, this is it! It’s a great website that discusses different stressors that can affect your cat’s well-being, and how to mitigate them to create a happy and enriched environment for your kitty.
